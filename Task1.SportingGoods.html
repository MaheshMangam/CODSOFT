ABOUT US

We are a team of entrepreneurs and sports persons based in Rajahmundry Sporting Goods, Andhra Pradesh, world famous manufacturing hub of sporting goods. We launched this online store to connect you with the famous and quality manufacturers of sports goods, especially from Andhra Pradesh, to ensure the original quality sports goods. We are authorized dealers of many leading brands. We guarantee 100% genuine and original quality branded sports products. Our mission is to help the Indian Player Community get direct access to the best quality sports goods delivered from the manufacturing hub of sporting goods.
